### Description (EN)
Fingenda is a personal finance app designed to make daily money management simple and effective.

What you can do:
- Log income and expenses quickly
- Review category-based spending analytics
- Create savings goals and track progress
- Manage installment and loan calculations
- Export and share records via receipt/PDF/Excel flows
- Get financial insights with Fingo Brain

Fingenda helps you stay in control of your budget with a modern interface, fast workflows, and a trust-focused experience.
